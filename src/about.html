ABOUT

Our Formula 1 analysis application is an application designed to look at the multiple factors of a race weekend and devise a reasonable estimation on how well the teams will perform that weekend. Through the use of two API's, an independent not for profit motosport API and a weather API, the app will allow for users to gain a good idea on the drivers performance for the selected race(s). The application is fantastic for F1 fans who want to dive further into the specific stats for the upcoming races, or just want to check up on a drivers recent form and analyise how a certain track has affected drivers in the past.
